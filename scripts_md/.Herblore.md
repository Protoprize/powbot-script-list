# Pow Herblore

### Your *All In One* needs for herblore!
This is an AIO herblore script for Oldschool Runescape. With a customisable task list to get you from 1-99 in a matter of hours while you go and do something else!

Any feedback please let me know on the methods below:
* [Script Thread](https://powbot.org/community/index.php?/topic/88-herblore-pro-all-herbs-all-potions-custom-cleaning-patterns-task-queue/)
* [Forums](https://powbot.org/community/index.php?/messenger/compose/&to=6)
* [Discord](https://discord.gg/YF8askQb)
